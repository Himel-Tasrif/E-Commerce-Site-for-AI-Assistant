Our Story

Innovation meets style. We're on a mission to bring the best athletic footwear to every athlete.

Our Mission

At Stride, we believe that every athlete deserves the best. Whether you're a professional runner chasing a world record or someone taking their first steps on a fitness journey, our shoes are engineered to help you perform at your peak.

Founded in 2010 by a team of passionate athletes and engineers, we set out to revolutionize the footwear industry by combining cutting-edge technology with timeless design. Today, we're proud to serve millions of athletes worldwide.

Our commitment goes beyond making great shoes. We're dedicated to sustainability, ethical manufacturing, and giving back to the communities that support us.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Innovation

We constantly push boundaries with new materials, technologies, and designs to help athletes perform better.

Community

We build connections through sport, supporting local communities and inspiring the next generation of athletes.

Sustainability

Committed to reducing our environmental footprint through recycled materials, renewable energy, and circular design.

Quality

Every shoe undergoes rigorous testing to ensure durability, comfort, and performance that lasts.

Integrity

Performance

Engineered for athletes who demand the best. Our shoes are tested by pros, proven on the field.

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped who we are today

2010

Founded

Stride was founded in a small garage by three former collegiate athletes who believed everyone deserves premium performance footwear.

2012

First Breakthrough

Launched our proprietary AirFlow cushioning technology, revolutionizing comfort in running shoes and winning our first industry award.

2015

Global Expansion

Opened flagship stores in New York, London, and Tokyo. Expanded distribution to 50+ countries worldwide.

2018

Sustainability Commitment

Pledged to use 50% recycled materials in all products by 2025. Launched the ReNew collection made from 100% recycled polyester.

2021

Smart Footwear Launch

Introduced the first connected running shoes with real-time performance tracking, coaching feedback, and injury prevention insights.

2024

Carbon Neutral Operations

Achieved carbon neutrality across all manufacturing facilities and corporate offices. Committed to net-zero by 2030.
